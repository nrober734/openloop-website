Six Schools, Two Countries, One Mission
Bringing Cities Closer

We are OpenLoop, an alliance of some of the world’s greatest universities joined together with the ultimate goal of revolutionizing the future of human transportation. We are competing in the SpaceX Hyperloop Design Competition to design, build, and test a scaled-down Hyperloop pod, and with the combined resources and talent of our six prestigious institutions, we’re uniquely poised to make significant contributions to the Hyperloop Project as a whole. All these contributions will be open-sourced to support Hyperloop’s continued development.
